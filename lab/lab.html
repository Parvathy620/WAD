Lab Safety Guidelines

⚠ Important Safety Rules
  • Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) as required for the specific lab.
  • No food, drinks, or chewing gum allowed inside the laboratory areas at any time.
  • Report any equipment malfunction, damage, or hazardous spills to lab personnel immediately.
  • Familiarize yourself with emergency procedures, including the location of first aid kits and fire extinguishers.
  • Follow all instructions from lab instructors and supervisors diligently.
  • Maintain a clean and organized workspace to prevent accidents and ensure efficient operations.
